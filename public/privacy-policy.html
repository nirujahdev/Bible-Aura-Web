Bible Aura ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what rights you have.

By using Bible Aura, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Optional profile picture, denomination or study preferences
  • Any information you include in your account profile

B. Bible Study Data

  • Bible AI chat messages and your study interactions
  • Reading progress, saved verses, and completed studies
  • Journal entries (encrypted and private to you)

C. Technical Data

  • Device and browser information
  • General location (optional, for content localization)
  • Cookies and analytics data for performance and preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Deliver and personalize Bible Aura services
  • Maintain and improve our AI models and platform
  • Communicate updates, service notices, or relevant content
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Bible AI Chat Privacy

Chat data is processed securely and never sold

AI training uses anonymized conversation patterns only

You can delete your conversation history anytime from your account

4. Data Sharing

We share data only in these situations:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, authentication, and payment processing (under binding agreements)
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or court order
  • Security: To protect users and the platform
  • Business Transfers: If Bible Aura merges or is acquired, with privacy safeguards

We do not sell personal data or share it with religious organizations without explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Strict internal access controls
  • Regular vulnerability testing and monitoring

You are responsible for maintaining your password confidentiality.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, delete, or export your personal data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting past lawful processing)

Requests can be sent to contact@bibleaura.xyz and will be addressed within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use:

  • Essential cookies (site functionality)
  • Preference cookies (save settings)
  • Analytics cookies (usage statistics)
  • Limited marketing cookies (non-invasive, relevant content)

You may control cookies in your browser, but disabling essential cookies may limit features.

8. Children's Privacy

Bible Aura is for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents should monitor minors' use of Bible Aura.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in other countries with legal protections such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy to reflect changes in our services or laws. Major changes will be notified via email or platform notices before taking effect.
